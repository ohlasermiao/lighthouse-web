About Lighthouse Club

Last updated: June 12, 2026 · 日本語 · 中文（main site）

What we are

Lighthouse Club is a paid online membership community operated by Syncvalue Inc.（シンクバリュー株式会社）, a company registered in Japan. Members pay a recurring membership fee for access to a private, moderated Discord community where they discuss everyday life, reading, learning, and skill-sharing with other members.

All content in the community is generated by members themselves. The operator provides the venue, community guidelines, and moderation only — we do not sell courses, do not provide consulting, and do not provide investment advice or any financial services.

Membership & delivery

  • Plan: USD 180 per 3 months (tax not included; applicable taxes vary by country and are shown at checkout).
  • Renewal: no automatic charges — renewal requires the member's own confirmation, and members receive a reminder before expiry. Membership can be stopped at any time.
  • Delivery: access is granted within 24 hours of payment via a Discord invitation link sent by email, together with the member role on our Discord server.
  • Refunds: a 7-day refund policy applies from first activation; details in our Terms of Service (Chinese) and Legal disclosure under the Specified Commercial Transactions Act (Japanese).
